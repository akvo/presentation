Agri Connect: Use Case Overview

Case Case Description Case Example General Challenges Tech Challenges Resources Needed
Use Case 1: Weather Information Service Reliable weather forecasting for optimal farming decisions. Jane relies on the service for precise timing of planting and irrigation. - Ensuring user trust and adoption.
- Integration with local communication practices. 		- Accuracy of weather data.
- Real-time data processing and dissemination. 		- Advanced weather prediction models.
- Integration APIs for local SMS/USSD/WhatsApp.
Use Case 2: Improving Access to Quality Inputs Providing reliable information on quality farming inputs. Abdoulaye seeks verified suppliers for his potato farm's inputs. - Verification and trust in input quality.
- Education on proper input usage. 		- Building a reliable supply chain platform.
- Verification systems for input quality. 		- Partnerships with input suppliers.
- Development of educational content.
Use Case 3: Access to Information on Pest and Disease Management Actionable information for managing new pests and diseases. Francis uses the system to identify and treat crop diseases efficiently. - Local relevance of information.
- Adoption of technology for pest/disease identification. 		- Data management for a large pest/disease image database.
- Development of accurate AI models for disease/pest recognition. 		- AI and machine learning expertise.
- Access to extensive labeled datasets.
Use Case 4: Empower the Agricultural Extension Officer Enhancing the efficiency and reach of agricultural extension officers. James leverages the tools to manage his advice to thousands of farmers. - Managing high farmer-to-officer ratios.
- Navigating bureaucratic obstacles. 		- Integration of AI for real-time decision support.
- Development of multilingual support systems. 		- AI development platforms and tools.
- Training for officers on new technologies.
